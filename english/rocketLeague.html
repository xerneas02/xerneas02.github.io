Rocket League Bot

During my second year of studies, I had the opportunity to participate in an exciting project that spanned two weeks, with an investment of over 150 hours. This project, undertaken as a group with a team of four, allowed each of us to actively engage in every aspect of the process, but I primarily led the technical implementation.

Our goal was to create a virtual agent capable of playing Rocket League at a beginner level. This challenge provided us with an excellent opportunity to explore and deepen our understanding of machine learning.

Over these intense two weeks, we worked together to develop and refine our agent's skills. Each team member contributed to all tasks, but I played a central role in the technical implementation.

For more information on our process, results, and reflections, I invite you to review our detailed report. Additionally, we created a dedicated website to share our experience and explain the steps involved in creating a Rocket League agent.

This project was a rewarding experience that allowed us to explore the exciting challenges of artificial intelligence in a gaming context like Rocket League.
