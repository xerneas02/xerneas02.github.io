Pokémon Red AI

During my third year of studies, I took part in a 120-hour project carried out in partnership with Sami Abdul-Salam.

Our goal was to develop an artificial intelligence capable of autonomously playing Pokémon Red. This challenge allowed us to explore reinforcement learning methods, game simulation, and the scalability of neural network architectures.

Throughout this intensive period, we implemented: game simulation using the PyBoy emulator, IA environment setup with Gym and Stable Baselines, reinforcement learning and neuroevolution training with NEAT, and performance analysis to evaluate results.

To learn more about our approach, findings, and insights, please consult our detailed project report and GitHub repository.

Technologies Used

  • Python
  • PyBoy (Game Boy emulation)
  • Gym & Stable Baselines
  • NEAT & Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN)
  • Reinforcement Learning (PPO, DQN, A2C)
