Chatbot for a Study Program

During my second-year internship at the Arctic University of Norway (UiT), I had the opportunity to work on an innovative project focused on the development and integration of a chatbot for the Master's program in Industrial Engineering. The main objective was to create an automated tool capable of providing precise and contextual responses to potential students' questions, thereby facilitating their access to information while reducing the workload of human agents.

To achieve this goal, I used the GPT-3.5 Turbo language model, an advanced natural language processing (NLP) technology capable of generating relevant responses based on the context of the conversation. The project began with an in-depth analysis of stakeholder needs, including interviews with students and administrative staff at UiT. This information was essential in guiding the chatbot's development to ensure it effectively met users' expectations.

The development process also included the creation of an intuitive user interface and the integration of the chatbot on UiT's website. Rigorous testing was conducted to evaluate the chatbot's effectiveness, and the results showed a high level of user satisfaction, with over 95% of responses deemed satisfactory.

A simulation carried out using AnyLogic software confirmed the chatbot's effectiveness, showing significant improvements compared to previous solutions in terms of response time reduction and decreased dropout rates. This project not only enriched my experience in developing NLP technologies but also enabled UiT to strengthen its information system, offering a better experience to prospective students.

For more details on this project and the technologies used, I invite you to consult my full internship report.
